How it works

From raw episode to a full content calendar in minutes.

Three steps. No third-party clip tools, no spreadsheets, no manual scheduling.

1

Drop in your episode

Upload your long-form video or audio. We transcribe it, find the best moments, and pull metadata automatically. Or auto-import from Google Drive or Dropbox.

2

AI does the work

Vidflow cuts 10–30 short clips, reframes them vertical, generates platform-specific captions in your brand voice, and creates supporting image and text posts.

3

Approve and schedule

Drag content onto your calendar, send clients a link to approve (no login needed), and publish across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, X, and Facebook.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

If you have a question we haven't answered, email landon@cosmicarcmedia.com — we read every one.

When does Vidflow launch?
Public launch is targeting later this year. Waitlist members get early access — typically a week of free use to try the platform before opening to the broader audience.
Is Vidflow only for podcasters?
No. Vidflow works for anyone with long-form video — podcasters, YouTubers, agencies, content marketers, course creators. Anywhere you have a recording and want to multiply it across platforms.
What platforms can I publish to?
YouTube (long-form and Shorts), Instagram (Reels, posts, carousels), TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn (posts and articles), X / Twitter, and podcast platforms via RSS (Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Overcast, etc.). More on the roadmap.
Do I need to install anything?
No. Vidflow runs entirely in the browser. Upload videos directly, or connect a Google Drive or Dropbox folder and we pull files automatically.
How does the AI handle my brand voice?
Import your existing YouTube channel and Vidflow analyzes your tone, vocabulary, recurring themes, and typical caption style. Every generated caption, post, and clip title is grounded in your voice — not generic AI-speak.
How much will it cost?
Pricing isn't finalized, but the goal is tiered plans starting affordable for individual creators and scaling to per-workspace pricing for agencies. Waitlist members will get founder pricing locked in for life.
Can I bring my own AI provider keys?
On higher tiers, yes. Bring-your-own-key (Gemini, OpenAI) lets you scale without per-clip costs — useful for agencies running high volume.
What about client approvals — do my clients need a Vidflow account?
No. You send them a single shareable link. They can review, approve, or comment on every piece of content with no signup required. The flow is built specifically for agencies that don't want to onboard every client to a new tool.
