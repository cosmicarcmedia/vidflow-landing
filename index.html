FAQ

Frequently asked questions

If you have a question we haven't answered, email landon@cosmicarcmedia.com — we read every one.

When does Vidflow launch?
We're rolling out early access to waitlist members now, then opening to the public later this year. Waitlist members get invited in batches before everyone else.
Is Vidflow only for agencies?
No. Vidflow works for anyone with long-form video or audio — we're just building and pricing it first for the people with the heaviest workload: agencies running content for multiple clients.
What platforms can I publish to?
YouTube (long-form and Shorts), Instagram (Reels, posts, carousels), TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn (posts and articles), X / Twitter, and podcast apps via RSS (Apple Podcasts, Spotify, and more). More on the roadmap.
How much will it cost?
Final pricing launches with tiers for creators and agencies — expect creator plans starting under $50/month and agency plans in the low hundreds. Waitlist members get discounted founder rates on day one, locked in for life.
Can I bring my own AI keys?
On higher-volume plans, yes. Agencies can plug in their own clipping and AI provider keys per workspace to control costs at scale — otherwise Vidflow's built-in engine handles it with nothing for you to set up.
Do my clients need a Vidflow account?
No. You send them a secure review link and they approve or comment on all content with no login. The flow is built specifically for agencies that don't want to onboard every client to a new tool.
Do I need to install anything?
No. Vidflow runs in the browser. Upload directly, or connect a Google Drive or Dropbox folder and we pull episodes for you.
