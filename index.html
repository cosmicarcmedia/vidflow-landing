FAQ

Frequently asked questions

If you have a question we haven't answered, use the contact form below — we read every one.

When does Vidflow launch?
We're rolling out early access to waitlist members soon, then opening to the public later this year. Waitlist members get invited in batches before anyone else.
Is Vidflow only for agencies?
No. It's made for anyone with long-form video or audio. We're just building and pricing it first for the people with the heaviest workload, agencies running content for multiple clients.
What platforms can I publish to?
YouTube (long-form and Shorts), Instagram (Reels, posts, carousels), TikTok, Facebook, LinkedIn (posts and articles), and X / Twitter. More on the roadmap.
How much will it cost?
We will be announcing pricing soon. Our founder rates will be the best deal, locked in forever.
Can I bring my own AI keys?
On higher-volume plans, yes. Agencies can plug in their own clipping and AI provider keys per workspace to control costs at scale, otherwise Vidflow's built-in engine handles it with nothing for you to set up.
Do my clients need a Vidflow account?
No. You send them a secure review link and they approve or comment on all content with no login. The flow is built specifically for agencies that don't want to onboard every client to a new tool.
Do I need to install anything?
No. Vidflow runs in the browser. Upload directly, or connect a Google Drive or Dropbox folder and we pull episodes for you.
