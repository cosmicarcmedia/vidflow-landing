Help / Plans, billing, and account

Manage your subscription

Updated July 9, 2026

Everything billing-related lives on one page in the app, including current pricing for every plan. This article covers starting a plan, switching plans, payment details, cancellation, and refunds.

#Open the Billing page

  1. Click your avatar in the app.
  2. Choose Account settings.
  3. Click Manage plan & billing.

The Billing & plan page shows your current plan and its status, your usage meters, and the plan catalog with pricing. If you are on a trial, a banner shows how many days remain.

#Start a paid plan

Every paid plan starts with a 7-day free trial. A card is required to start the trial.

  1. On the Billing page, pick a plan.
  2. Click Start 7-day trial.
  3. Complete checkout. Payment is handled by Stripe, and you can enter a promotion code during checkout.

#Switch plans

To upgrade or downgrade, click Switch to [plan name] on the plan you want. The change applies to your existing subscription immediately, and prorated charges or credits appear on your next invoice. Switching never creates a second subscription.

On the Agency plan you can also adjust extra brand spaces from the plan card and confirm with Update brand spaces. Extra spaces are billed as an add-on quantity on the same subscription.

Note

Note: if your plan shows a green Complimentary badge, Vidflow granted your account free full access and no payment is needed.

#Update your card or view invoices

Click Manage billing to open the secure Stripe billing portal. From there you can update your payment method, view past invoices, and cancel your subscription.

#Cancel your subscription

Cancel from the Stripe billing portal via Manage billing. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, and you keep access to paid features until then.

Canceling does not delete your account or content. To delete data, see Your data and privacy.

#Refunds

You can request a full refund of your first subscription charge within 14 days of that charge. Email support@vidflow.io from your account's email address.

After those 14 days, fees are non-refundable, and there are no prorated refunds for cancellations except where the law requires them.
