Help / Plans, billing, and account

Manage your subscription

Updated July 10, 2026

Everything billing-related lives on one page in the app, including current pricing for every plan. Reach for this page to start a plan, switch plans, update payment details, cancel, or request a refund.

#How it works

Vidflow does not run its own billing ledger, Stripe is the system of record for your card, invoices, and subscription state. When you start a trial or switch plans, Vidflow talks to Stripe to create or update the subscription, then a webhook syncs the result (plan, status, trial end date, brand space count) back into your account so the app always reflects what Stripe actually has on file. Because of that sync, changes made in the Stripe portal (canceling, updating a card) show up back in Vidflow automatically, no separate step needed.

#Open the Billing page

  1. Click your avatar in the app.
  2. Choose Account settings.
  3. Click Manage plan & billing.

The Billing & plan page shows your current plan and its status, your usage meters, and the plan catalog with pricing. If you are on a trial, a banner shows how many days remain.

#Start a paid plan

Every paid plan starts with a 7-day free trial. A card is required to start the trial.

  1. On the Billing page, pick a plan.
  2. Click Start 7-day trial.
  3. Complete checkout. Payment is handled by Stripe, and you can enter a promotion code during checkout.

#Switch plans

To upgrade or downgrade, click Switch to [plan name] on the plan you want. The change applies to your existing subscription immediately, and prorated charges or credits appear on your next invoice. Switching never creates a second subscription, an existing subscriber is always updated in place rather than sent through checkout again.

On the Agency plan you can also adjust extra brand spaces from the plan card with the stepper and confirm with Update brand spaces. Extra spaces are billed as an add-on quantity on the same subscription.

Note

Note: if your plan shows a green Complimentary badge, Vidflow granted your account free full access and no payment is needed.

#Update your card or view invoices

Click Manage billing to open the secure Stripe billing portal. From there you can update your payment method, view past invoices, and cancel your subscription.

#Cancel your subscription

Cancel from the Stripe billing portal via Manage billing. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period, and you keep access to paid features until then.

Canceling does not delete your account or content. To delete data, see Your data and privacy.

#Refunds

You can request a full refund of your first subscription charge within 14 days of that charge. Email support@vidflow.io from your account's email address.

After those 14 days, fees are non-refundable, and there are no prorated refunds for cancellations except where the law requires them.

#Troubleshooting

I switched plans but the page still shows the old plan. The Billing page refetches automatically after a successful switch or checkout return; if it still looks stale, refresh the page. If it persists more than a few minutes, contact support@vidflow.io, the Stripe sync may have failed silently.

Manage billing button is missing. That button only appears once you have an active Stripe customer record, which happens the first time you start a trial or paid plan. On the Free plan with no billing history yet, there is nothing to manage.

Billing isn't fully switched on yet banner. Vidflow shows this if Stripe is not fully configured on the current environment. You can still browse plans; checkout opens as soon as billing is live.
