Finding your way around

Updated July 9, 2026

Once you are inside a workspace, everything in Vidflow is reachable from a left-hand navigation rail, a global Make Content button, and a notifications bell.

#The navigation rail

The rail has seven sections:

  • Home: opens your Dashboard, with your upcoming schedule, quick actions, and recent activity.
  • Content Library: All Content, Bulk Actions, and Auto-Import.
  • Publish: Calendar and Schedules under Schedule, Approvals under Review.
  • Magic Tools: Clip Generator, Idea Generator, Idea Library, Script Generator, and Script Library under Create; Magic Text Posts and Blog Post under Distribute.
  • Flows: opens the Flows automation builder directly.
  • Analytics: Overview and Top Content.
  • Settings: opens Workspace Settings.

Sections marked as panels expand to show their items; Flows opens straight to its own page.

#The Make Content button

The Make Content button in the global header is the fastest way to start something new, from anywhere in the app. It opens a menu with:

  • Upload Episode, to upload a video or audio file as a new episode.
  • Import Episodes, to import existing content, such as from YouTube.
  • Upload Content, to add a finished piece of content directly to your library.
  • Magic Text Posts, to jump straight to generating text posts.
  • Create Image Post, to start a new image-based post.
  • Bulk Upload, to add many pieces of content at once.

#The notifications bell

The bell in the header tracks jobs running in the background, most commonly transcription. When an episode is transcribing, it shows up here as a "Transcription" job, and clicking it takes you to your episodes so you can check on progress. Use it as your at-a-glance signal for whether a video is ready to work with yet.

#The Dashboard

Home opens your Dashboard, which includes a schedule and upcoming posts card with a Schedule a post shortcut, a Quick Actions card, a Published by Platform breakdown, and other activity cards. If you have not selected a workspace yet, the Dashboard shows "No Workspace Selected" until you pick or create one.

New accounts also see a short setup walkthrough here, a step-by-step dialog that links directly to brand basics, target audience, your brand sheet, AI and voice settings, image style, and connecting accounts. You can skip it and reopen it later from the getting-started checklist on the Dashboard.

#Getting help

Open the Help page from Settings for quick links to common features, a short FAQ, and a "Still stuck?" link that opens an email to support@vidflow.io.
