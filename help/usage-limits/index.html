Usage limits and monthly allowances

Updated July 9, 2026

Vidflow meters three things each month: episodes, AI generations, and clip generations. This page explains how the meters work, what counts against each one, and your options when you reach a cap.

#How allowances work

  • Allowances reset at the start of each calendar month (UTC).
  • Episodes are metered per brand space. Your account's monthly total is your plan's per-brand allowance multiplied by the brand spaces on your subscription.
  • AI generations and clip generations are account-wide pools. All of your brand spaces draw from the same two pools.

Your plan sets the numbers:

PlanEpisodes per month per brand spaceAI generations per monthClip generations per month
Free1203
Starter215030
Pro8600120
Agency82,500500

See Plans and features for the full plan matrix, including seats and connected account slots.

#What counts as an episode

Every long-form source you add counts as one episode, whichever way it arrives: a direct upload, a YouTube import, or an auto-import from Google Drive or Dropbox.

#What counts as an AI generation

Each AI action draws from the same pool:

  • AI writing: captions, text posts, ideas, scripts, and articles.
  • AI image generation.
  • Transcription of an uploaded file.

Batch actions count once per item generated. Generating ten posts in one run uses ten AI generations, not one.

#What counts as a clip generation

Every clip you generate counts as one against the pool.

#Connected account slots

Your plan also caps connected social accounts per brand space. Reconnecting an account that is already linked never uses a new slot.

#Tracking your usage

Open the Billing page: click your avatar, choose Account settings, then Manage plan & billing. The meters near the top show Episodes this month, AI generations, and Clips against your plan's limits.

#When you hit a cap

The action stops, and Vidflow shows a message naming the limit you reached and pointing you to the Billing page. Content you already created is not affected.

Your options:

  1. Wait for the monthly reset.
  2. Upgrade your plan. Changes apply to your existing subscription right away, with prorated charges or credits. See Manage your subscription.
  3. On Agency, add brand spaces to raise your total episode allowance.
