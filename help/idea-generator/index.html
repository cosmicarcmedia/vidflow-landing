Idea Generator and Idea Library

Updated July 9, 2026

The Idea Generator brainstorms content ideas in your brand voice. The Idea Library keeps the ones you save, tracks which you have used, and feeds them straight into the Script Generator.

Both live under Magic Tools in the left rail: Idea Generator, with Idea Library beneath it.

#Generate ideas

  1. Open Idea Generator.
  2. Pick a Number of ideas: 3, 6, or 10 (6 is the default).
  3. Add a Topic or theme (optional), or leave it blank to generate ideas based on your brand voice and past content.
  4. Click Generate Ideas.

Two ways to steer the batch:

  • Watch trending suggests angles to explore. Suggestions appear as pills; click one to add it to your topic. If nothing comes back, run it again.
  • Start from a top performer appears once your analytics show top episodes. Pick an episode to riff on and the generator seeds itself from that episode's transcript.

Each generated idea appears as a card. Click Save to keep it in your Idea Library, or Outline to expand a quick outline before you decide.

Note: If your brand voice is not set up, the page shows a banner and the AI writes in a generic voice. See Set up your brand voice. Each generation also counts toward your plan's monthly AI allowance. See Usage limits and monthly allowances.

#Work the library

Open Idea Library. Ideas are split into Unused and Used tabs; an idea moves to Used once you turn it into a script.

For each saved idea you can:

  • Script: open the Script Generator seeded with this idea.
  • Expand: have the AI develop the idea further.
  • Outline: show or hide the idea's outline.
  • Mark as used or Mark as unused from the idea's menu.
  • Delete: remove the idea.

Saved ideas also appear in the Script Generator's side panel, where you can pull them into a script as you write. See Script Generator.
