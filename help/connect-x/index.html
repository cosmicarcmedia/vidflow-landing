Help / Connecting social accounts

Connect X

Updated July 9, 2026

Connect X (Twitter) to publish text and media posts from Vidflow.

#Connect steps

  1. Open Workspace Settings.
  2. Click the Connections tab.
  3. Click X (Twitter).
  4. Sign in on X's page and approve access. X always shows its login screen here, so you can connect a different or additional account even if one is already signed in on your device.
  5. You are returned to Vidflow with X listed as connected.

#What Vidflow can publish

Text and media posts to your connected X account, from Post now on library content and from scheduled dispatch on both content items and legacy queue posts.

#Limits enforced

  • Post text: 280 characters. Scheduled content items are automatically sliced to this length.
  • Media: 512MB hard cap. Video uploads process asynchronously on X's side, which can take up to about 2 minutes before the post finishes.

#Token lifetime and reconnect

X issues rotating refresh tokens, and Vidflow refreshes them automatically in the background, deduplicated so that overlapping refresh attempts cannot burn through the token family. Most connections need no attention.

Note

Note: Posting media requires the media.write permission scope. If your X account was connected before Vidflow requested that scope, media posts will not work until you reconnect. Text-only posts are unaffected.

If X revokes the authorization outright, Vidflow pauses that account and emails you. Reconnecting resumes anything waiting on your schedule.

#Troubleshooting

Media posts fail but text posts work fine. Reconnect your X account so Vidflow can request the media.write scope. This is a one-time fix.

My video post is taking a while to appear. X processes video uploads asynchronously. Give it a couple of minutes before treating it as stuck.

My media was rejected for size. Confirm the file is under 512MB. Vidflow enforces this cap before attempting the upload.

X connection stopped working entirely. Check the Connections tab for a reconnect prompt and sign in again.
