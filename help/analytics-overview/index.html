Analytics overview

Updated July 9, 2026

The Analytics page tracks your content output and audience growth for the current workspace. This article covers when data appears and what each card measures.

#When data appears

Analytics fill in as you connect social accounts and publish posts. A new workspace shows a "We're still collecting your data" card with a Connect accounts button until there is something to chart.

Two things feed the page:

  • Publishing through Vidflow. Post now, scheduled posts, and posts published by flows all count. Posts you publish directly on a platform, outside Vidflow, do not appear in the publishing cards.
  • Connected accounts. Vidflow records follower counts for your connected accounts daily, which powers the Audience card.

#Pick a period

The period selector offers Last 7 days, Last 30 days, and Last 90 days. It defaults to 30.

The period scopes the Audience change figures, the Posts card, and the Publishing Activity chart. The Total Episodes, Total Clips, and Published Posts cards are workspace totals and do not change with the period.

#What each card shows

  • Audience: current follower count per connected platform, with the change over the selected period.
  • Total Episodes: every episode in the workspace.
  • Total Clips: every clip generated in the workspace.
  • Published Posts: everything published from this workspace.
  • Posts: posts published inside the selected period. The card label names the window, for example "Posts (last 30d)".
  • Publishing Activity: a bar chart of posts per day across the selected period. Until you publish inside the window it reads "No publishing activity yet".
  • Platform Distribution: how your published posts split across platforms, as counts and percentages.
  • Top Episodes by Clips: your episodes ranked by how many clips were generated from each. Click an episode to open it. The Top Content link in the rail jumps here.
  • Content Breakdown: your posts grouped by content type.

#Troubleshooting

  • The whole page shows "We're still collecting your data": connect at least one social account and publish a post. See Connect your social accounts.
  • The chart is empty but other cards have numbers: nothing was published inside the selected period. Widen the period or check your schedule.
  • "Select a workspace to view analytics.": pick a workspace first, then reopen the page.
