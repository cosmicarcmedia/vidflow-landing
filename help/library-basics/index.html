Content Library basics

Updated July 9, 2026

The Content Library holds everything in a workspace: text posts, images, clips, articles, and the episodes they came from. This article shows you how to find things fast and what the status labels mean.

Open it from the rail: click Content Library, then All Content.

#Tabs

Three tabs split the library by what you are looking at:

  • All Content shows every item in the workspace.
  • Episodes shows only your long-form source videos.
  • Clips shows only clips generated from episodes.

Episodes are sources, not posts. See Episodes vs. library content for how the two relate.

If the library is empty, the page offers shortcuts such as Upload Video, Import from YouTube, and Magic Text Posts to create your first content.

#Views and sorting

The view button on the left of the filter row switches between Grid View, Compact View, and List View.

The sort menu next to it orders items by Date Created, Title A-Z, Unused First, Unapproved First, or Media Type.

#Filters

The same sort menu also holds the filters. They stack with search:

  • All Types, or one content type: Text, Articles, Images, Clips, Episodes.
  • All Tags, or one of your tags. The Manage Tags button beside the menu creates and edits tags. See Tags.
  • All Approval States, Needs Approval, or Approved.

A separate From episode select narrows the library to content created from one source episode. Pick All sources to clear it.

#Search

The search box matches titles by default. The toggle beside it switches scope:

  • Titles searches item titles only.
  • Full also searches captions and metadata.

#Content statuses

Every post, image, and clip carries one status:

  • Draft: a working copy. It does not publish anywhere.
  • Queued: the item sits in a queue and goes out at the queue's next open time slot.
  • Scheduled: the item has a specific publish date and time.
  • Published: the item has already been posted.

Change a status from the item's editor. See Edit a post. Once a post is dispatched, you may also see publishing and failed states; see Post statuses and failed posts.

#Approval states

Approval is a separate dimension from status. It tracks client review, not publishing:

  • Draft: not submitted for review.
  • Needs approval: submitted and waiting on a reviewer. The editor shows a Pending Approval badge.
  • Approved: cleared by the reviewer.
  • Rejected: turned down by the reviewer.

Approval by itself publishes nothing. An approved item still goes out through its status (queued or scheduled) or through Post Now. See Approval status in your library.

#Loading more

The library loads a window of items at a time. Click Load more content at the bottom to extend it. A counter shows how many items you are viewing out of the total.

Note

Note: While a Flow is generating content, a banner appears at the top of the library and new posts show up automatically as they finish. See Flows overview.
