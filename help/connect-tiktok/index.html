Connect TikTok

Updated July 9, 2026

Connect TikTok to publish and schedule videos to your TikTok account directly from Vidflow.

Beta note

Beta note: TikTok posts currently publish as private (visible only to you) while the TikTok app review completes. Vidflow marks the post and emails you once a post is affected, and switches to normal publishing automatically when the review finishes.

#Connect steps

  1. Open Workspace Settings.
  2. Click the Connections tab.
  3. Click TikTok.
  4. Sign in on TikTok's page and approve access.
  5. You are returned to Vidflow with TikTok listed as connected.

#What Vidflow can publish

Vidflow publishes video posts to TikTok, both from Post now on library content and from scheduled dispatch. A video file is required, TikTok cannot post text-only or image-only content.

#The TikTok settings panel

Every TikTok post, whether posted now or scheduled, includes a required settings panel:

  • Posting as {your TikTok name}, confirming which account will post.
  • Who can view this post: the exact choices come from your TikTok account's own settings and typically include Public, Friends, Followers, and Only you. Vidflow only offers the audiences your TikTok account allows.
  • Allow comments toggle.
  • Allow Duet and Allow Stitch toggles (video posts only).
  • Disclose video content: turn this on if the post promotes a brand, product, or service, then choose Your brand (you are promoting yourself or your own business) or Branded content (a paid partnership or promoting another brand).

Branded content can never be posted privately. If disclosure is set to branded content, you must choose an audience other than Only you.

#Limits enforced

  • Title: 150 characters.
  • A video file is required for every TikTok post.
  • You must choose who can view the post before it can be scheduled or posted.
  • If disclosure is turned on, you must pick Your brand or Branded content, or turn disclosure back off.

#Token lifetime and reconnect

TikTok access tokens last about 24 hours, and Vidflow refreshes them automatically roughly 30 minutes before they expire, so day-to-day use needs no action from you. If TikTok definitively rejects a refresh, for example because the authorization was revoked on TikTok's side, Vidflow pauses publishing to that account and emails you. Reconnect from the Connections tab and anything waiting on your schedule resumes.

#Troubleshooting

My post published as private and I did not choose that. This is the TikTok review-period behavior described above. Vidflow notes it on the post and by email, and it clears up once the app review completes.

Branded content will not let me choose Only you. This is expected. TikTok does not allow branded content to be private. Choose a different audience, or turn off disclosure if the post is not actually a paid partnership.

TikTok posting suddenly stopped working. Check the Connections tab for a reconnect prompt. TikTok token refresh failures show as "TikTok token refresh failed. Reconnect your TikTok account in Workspace Settings → Connected Accounts."

My comments, duet, or stitch toggle looks locked. Vidflow shows a hint when your TikTok account itself has that interaction type turned off. Change it in your TikTok app settings, then it will be available in Vidflow.
