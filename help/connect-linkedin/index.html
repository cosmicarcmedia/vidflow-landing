Connect LinkedIn

Updated July 10, 2026

Connect LinkedIn to publish posts to your LinkedIn profile from Vidflow.

#How it works

Vidflow posts through LinkedIn's UGC Posts API using the person URN captured when you connected. A video post attaches your media as a ready-to-view asset alongside your text; a post with no video, or with an image, publishes as text only, since there's no verified image-attachment path today. LinkedIn's stored token has no background refresh, so publishing checks the token's expiry before every attempt and fails fast with a clear reconnect message rather than surfacing LinkedIn's own generic API error.

#Connect steps

  1. Open Workspace Settings.
  2. Click the Connections tab.
  3. Click LinkedIn.
  4. Sign in on LinkedIn's page and approve access.
  5. You are returned to Vidflow with LinkedIn listed as connected.

#What Vidflow can publish

Text and video posts to your connected LinkedIn profile, from Post now on library content and from scheduled dispatch on both content items and legacy queue posts.

#Limits enforced

  • Title: 200 characters.

#Token lifetime and reconnect

LinkedIn tokens last roughly 60 days. Expect a periodic reconnect prompt as that window closes. When LinkedIn expires the authorization, Vidflow pauses publishing to that account and emails you explaining what happened. Reconnecting from the Connections tab picks back up anything waiting on your schedule.

#Get better results

  • Expect the ~60-day reconnect and plan around it, especially for a client workspace you don't check daily. Put a recurring reminder on your own calendar so LinkedIn posting for that workspace never goes quiet for days before anyone notices.
  • Keep titles under 200 characters from the draft stage, not as an afterthought. Vidflow enforces the cap, but writing to it from the start avoids an ugly last-second trim of your strongest line.
  • Attach video when you want more than a text post to stand out. LinkedIn supports both from Vidflow; a video post to a professional feed tends to hold attention longer than text alone.

#Troubleshooting

LinkedIn posting stopped working after a couple months. This is the expected reconnect cycle. Reconnect from the Connections tab, no data is lost.

I reconnected but posts are still failing. Confirm the reconnect completed and LinkedIn shows as connected rather than pending. If it still fails, contact support with the timestamp of the failed post.
