Help / Plans, billing, and account

Plans and features

Updated July 9, 2026

Vidflow has four plans: Free, Starter, Pro, and Agency. This page is the reference for what each plan includes. For current pricing, open the Billing page in the app.

#Plan limits

Every allowance below resets monthly. Usage limits and monthly allowances explains how the meters work.

PlanBrand spacesWorking seatsEpisodes per monthAI generations per monthClip generations per monthConnected social accounts
Free1112031
Starter112150303

Agency1, expandable1082,500500Unlimited

How to read the table:

  • Brand spaces are workspaces. One brand space holds one brand's content, connected accounts, and settings. Everyone gets their first workspace, including on the Free plan. Agency owners can purchase additional brand spaces as an add-on.
  • Working seats are admin and brand manager seats per brand space. The workspace owner counts as one seat. Client reviewer seats are free and unlimited on every plan.
  • Episodes per month is a per-brand-space allowance. On Agency, each additional brand space adds its own episode allowance to your account total.
  • AI generations and clip generations are account-wide pools shared across all your brand spaces.
  • Connected social accounts are counted per brand space.

#Features by plan

Every plan includes the core workflow: upload episodes, generate clips and AI content within your monthly allowances, connect social accounts, and schedule posts.

Paid plans add features on top:

  • Starter and up: client approval links. Share a review link so clients can approve content without a Vidflow login.
  • Pro and up: everything in Starter, plus Flows automation, bulk actions, per-platform captions and outputs, content recycling and optimal posting times, AI voice profile from YouTube, advanced long-form generators, client connect links, and team seats.
  • Agency: everything in Pro, plus the ability to purchase extra brand spaces for running multiple brands.

#Trials and pricing

Every paid plan starts with a 7-day free trial. A card is required to start the trial.

Current pricing lives on the Billing page in the app: click your avatar, choose Account settings, then Manage plan & billing. Manage your subscription walks through upgrades, downgrades, and cancellation.
