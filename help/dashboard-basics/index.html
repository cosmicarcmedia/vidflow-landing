Help / Getting started

The dashboard at a glance

Updated July 10, 2026

The Dashboard is what you land on when you open a workspace's Home. It is a single-page overview of where your content stands: what you've made, what's about to publish, what needs your attention, and a couple of nudges to help you finish setting up.

#How it works

Every card on the Dashboard reads live data scoped to your current workspace, there's nothing to configure. Switching workspaces refreshes every card to that workspace's own numbers. If no workspace is selected yet, the Dashboard shows a No Workspace Selected message with a shortcut to create one instead of any of the cards below.

#What's on the Dashboard

  • Stat cards. Four numbers up top: Total Episodes, Total Content, Pending Approval, and Scheduled Posts. Each one links straight to the relevant screen (episodes in your library, the full library, Approvals, or the Calendar). A small alert icon appears on Pending Approval when it's above zero.
  • Getting started checklist. A dismissible card that tracks six setup steps, brand voice, connecting an account, creating content, building a schedule, scheduling a post, and setting up approvals, with a progress bar and a Replay setup guide link. It disappears on its own once every step is complete, or you can dismiss it early with the X.
  • Upcoming Scheduled Posts. The next several posts headed to your calendar, with their platforms and how soon they go out. Click any row, or View Calendar, to jump to the full Calendar.
  • Unread Comments. Client and teammate comments left on approvals that you haven't seen yet, with a Mark all viewed shortcut and a direct link from each comment to the post, clip, or approval it belongs to.
  • Posts Published, Last 7 Days. A stacked bar chart of what published each day, broken out by content type (image, text, clip, video, carousel, episode).
  • Quick Actions. Shortcuts to Upload Episode, Manage Clips, Review Approvals, View Calendar, and Manage Queues, with a badge showing your pending approval count.
  • Published by Platform. A count of published posts per connected platform, once you have at least one. This card is hidden entirely until there's data to show.
  • Tip of the day. A small, quiet, dismissible tip card that rotates through practical suggestions. Dismissing one hides it for good; a new one can still show up on your next visit until you've dismissed them all.

#Get better results

  • Treat the getting-started checklist as a punch list, not decoration, the six steps it tracks (brand voice, an account, content, a schedule, a scheduled post, approvals) are the same things that make every other part of Vidflow work better.
  • Use Replay setup guide any time you want a refresher on where each setting lives, it reopens the same step-by-step tour without redoing the onboarding wizard.
  • Check Unread Comments regularly if you run client approvals, it's the fastest way to see feedback without opening the Approvals page directly.
  • Watch the Posts Published chart for gaps, a flat week is usually a sign your calendar or a queue ran dry, not that publishing failed.

#Troubleshooting

The Dashboard says "No Workspace Selected." Create or select a workspace first. See Workspaces and brand spaces.

A card shows zero even though I know I have content. Stat cards are scoped to the currently selected workspace. Double check you're in the right one if you manage more than one brand.

The getting-started checklist won't go away. It only auto-hides once all six steps are complete. Dismiss it manually with the X if you'd rather not finish every step right now, you can bring it back with Replay setup guide whenever you want.
