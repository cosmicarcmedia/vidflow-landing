Account security

Updated July 9, 2026

Protect your Vidflow account with two-factor authentication (2FA) and review every device where you are signed in.

To open the Security page, click your avatar, choose Account settings, then click Two-factor & active sessions.

#How 2FA works in Vidflow

Two-factor authentication adds a second step to your sign-in: a 6-digit code from an authenticator app.

Important

Important: 2FA applies when you sign in with your email and password. If you sign in with Google, that sign-in is protected by your Google account's own security settings instead.

#Turn on two-factor authentication

  1. Open the Security page.
  2. Click Enable two-factor.
  3. Scan the QR code with your authenticator app.
  4. Enter the 6-digit code from the app to confirm.
  5. Save your recovery codes. Copy them or download the .txt file.
Important

Important: recovery codes are shown once. Each code works one time if you lose access to your authenticator app. Store them somewhere safe.

After setup, signing in with your password prompts for a 6-digit code. You can enter a recovery code in its place.

#Manage recovery codes

The Security page shows how many recovery codes you have left. Click Regenerate recovery codes to get a fresh set. You confirm with a current code, and the old set stops working immediately.

#Turn off two-factor authentication

Click Disable 2FA on the Security page and confirm with a current authenticator code or a recovery code.

#Active sessions

The Active sessions card lists every device signed in to your account, with your current session marked.

  • Click Sign out next to a session to end it on that device.
  • Click Sign out everywhere to end every session on every device.
Note

Note: setting a new password also signs you out everywhere else. See Signing in and resetting your password.

#Troubleshooting

The code did not match. Codes rotate, so enter the code your authenticator app is showing right now and try again.

Lost your authenticator device. Sign in with one of your recovery codes, then disable 2FA and re-enable it with your new device. Each recovery code works once.

No recovery codes left and no authenticator. Email support@vidflow.io from your account's email address.
