Getting access to Vidflow

Updated July 9, 2026

Vidflow is currently invite-only, with new accounts onboarded in waves from the waitlist.

#How access works today

There is no open signup. Every sign-in is checked against an invite list, whether you sign in with Google or with email and password. If your email is not on that list, you land on the waitlist page instead of the app.

Beta note

Beta note: Vidflow is in an invite-only beta. Access opens in waves as capacity allows.

#If you try to sign in without an invite

If you attempt to sign in with an email that has not been invited, you are redirected to a waitlist page. Depending on your situation, you will see one of two messages:

  • "Vidflow is invite only." if your email has never been seen before.
  • "Your account has not been approved yet." if your email was already caught by the process once.

Either way, the page lets you join the waitlist right there. Submitting the form always confirms your spot, whether or not your email was already known to Vidflow.

#After you join the waitlist

There is no published timeline for when waitlisted accounts get invited. Access is granted in waves, and the Vidflow team decides when to pull from the waitlist based on capacity.

#When you get invited

Once you are approved, you will receive an email with the subject line "Your Vidflow access is ready." The email confirms you have been granted access and tells you to sign in at app.vidflow.io using that same email address.

After you receive that email

  1. Go to app.vidflow.io.
  2. Sign in using the exact email address the invite email was sent to, either with Google or with an email and password you set up. See Signing in and resetting your password.
  3. On your first sign-in, you land on the Brand Spaces screen. Every account includes one workspace at no cost, regardless of plan. See Workspaces and brand spaces.
  4. From there, follow the quickstart to set up your brand voice and get your first video moving through the pipeline.

#Troubleshooting

I signed in with a different email than the one that was invited. Access is tied to the exact email address on the invite. Sign in with that same address, or ask whoever invited you to add your other email as well.

I never got the invite email. Email delivery is not guaranteed to arrive instantly, and it can land in spam. If you were told you have access but cannot find the email, try signing in directly at app.vidflow.io with the invited email address. If that fails, contact support@vidflow.io.

I was invited by a teammate to join their workspace, not the platform waitlist. That is a different kind of invite. See Invite team members for how workspace-level invites work once you already have platform access.
