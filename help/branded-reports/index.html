Branded client reports

Updated July 10, 2026

Generate a client-ready performance report styled with your own branding, then save it as a PDF. The report is built from the same data as the Analytics page.

#How it works

Clicking Branded report builds a standalone HTML page server-side, using the same numbers the Analytics page shows for the period you have selected, and stamps it with your workspace's logo and brand color instead of Vidflow's. The page opens in a new tab and triggers your browser's print dialog automatically, so "export" is really "print to PDF" using the browser you already have open. Nothing is uploaded or emailed; the file only exists once you save it from the print dialog.

#What the report includes

The report carries your workspace logo (or your workspace initial if no logo is set), your workspace name, and your brand color. The header reads "Performance Report" with the covered period and the date it was generated.

Sections:

  • Overview: posts published, scheduled, awaiting approval, episodes, clips created, and total content.
  • Audience: followers and change per platform.
  • Published by platform: how published posts split across platforms.
  • Top episodes: your top episodes ranked by clips generated.
  • Content pipeline: draft, scheduled, and published counts.

The footer shows your workspace name and a small "Prepared with Vidflow" line.

Note

Note: The report does not include the per-post view, like, comment, and share numbers from Post Performance. It covers publishing volume, audience, and pipeline; for engagement on individual posts, share that section directly or screenshot it. See Post performance.

Set your logo and brand color in your workspace's brand settings; the same branding covers the client approval portal. See Set up your brand voice.

#Generate the report

  1. Open Analytics from the app rail.
  2. Pick the period the report should cover: Last 7 days, Last 30 days, or Last 90 days.
  3. Click Branded report.
  4. The report opens in a new browser tab, and the print dialog appears automatically.
  5. In the print dialog, choose your browser's save-as-PDF destination and save.
Important

Important: The report opens as a pop-up. If your browser blocks it, Vidflow shows "Allow pop-ups to open the report." Allow pop-ups for app.vidflow.io and click Branded report again.

#Get better results

  • Set your logo and brand color before you ever need a report, not the morning of a client call. Both live in Brand Settings and apply retroactively to every report you generate after that.
  • Match the period to what you're actually reporting on. A monthly retainer client usually wants Last 30 days; a quarterly check-in reads better with Last 90 days.
  • In the print dialog, turn on "Background graphics" (the exact wording varies by browser) so your brand color and logo actually render in the saved PDF instead of printing as plain black and white.
  • Generate the report right before the call rather than saving an old copy. Since it pulls live Analytics data, a report from last week will already be stale.

#Troubleshooting

  • Nothing opened when you clicked Branded report: your browser blocked the pop-up. Allow pop-ups for app.vidflow.io and try again.
  • You closed the print dialog before saving: the report tab is still open. Press Cmd+P (Mac) or Ctrl+P (Windows) in that tab to reopen it.
  • The report looks thin: report data fills in the same way Analytics does, as accounts connect and posts publish. See Analytics overview.
