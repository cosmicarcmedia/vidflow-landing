Quickstart: one video to a week of content

Updated July 9, 2026

This is the golden path through Vidflow: from your first sign-in to a full week of content sitting on your calendar. Follow it once, in order, and every later visit is faster.

Note

Note: The dashboard also includes a short in-app setup guide that walks through steps 2 to 4 below with direct links to each screen. Use whichever you prefer, they cover the same ground.

#The path

  1. Get invited and sign in. Vidflow is invite-only during the beta. Once you have access, sign in with Google or with an email and password. See Getting access to Vidflow and Signing in and resetting your password.
  2. Confirm your workspace. Your first sign-in lands you on the Brand Spaces screen, and your first workspace is included on every plan. If you are setting up for a single brand, you likely already have what you need; agencies create one workspace per client brand. See Workspaces and brand spaces.
  3. Set up your brand voice. Open Workspace Settings and fill in your Brand Profile: brand name, target audience, and your brand sheet. This is what keeps the AI from writing in a generic voice. See Set up your brand voice.
  4. Upload or import your first video. Use the Upload Episode option from the Make Content menu to upload a file, or import an existing YouTube video. Either way, this becomes your first episode. See Upload a video or audio file and Import from YouTube.
  5. Wait for the transcript. Vidflow transcribes your episode automatically after upload or import. Everything generated from this episode, clips, captions, posts, depends on that transcript being ready, so give it a few minutes and watch the notifications bell for progress. See Transcripts.
  6. Generate clips. Open the Clip Generator, pick your episode, and generate a batch of short clips with captions already burned in. See Generate clips from a long video.
  7. Generate Magic Text Posts. From the same episode, generate a batch of quote, insight, and tip posts written in your brand voice. See Magic Text Posts.
  8. Connect a social account. Go to Workspace Settings, Connections, and connect at least one platform so Vidflow has somewhere to publish. See Connect your social accounts.
  9. Schedule your content, or post now. Send your new clips and posts to the Calendar to spread them across the week, or publish one immediately with Post Now. See The Calendar, Schedules and queues, and Post now.
  10. Optional: send it for approval instead. If you are creating content for a client or a team lead, create a shareable approval link instead of scheduling directly, so they can approve or comment before anything goes out. See Create and share an approval link.

#What you have at the end

One episode has become a batch of clips and posts, all in your brand voice, either scheduled across the coming week or waiting on a client's approval. Repeat from step 4 with your next video.
