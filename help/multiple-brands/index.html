Running multiple brands

Updated July 10, 2026

Agencies running content for more than one client use a separate workspace for each brand. Reach for this article when you are onboarding a second client, deciding whether to upgrade to Agency, or moving content between two workspaces you already own.

#How it works

Every workspace is its own isolated row of data: its own episodes, connected accounts, brand voice, and members. Adding a brand space does not "share" one account across brands, it gives your login access to a second, completely separate workspace. Plan limits (episodes, AI generations, clips) are pooled at the account level and explained in Usage limits and monthly allowances; everything else about a workspace stays private to it.

#One workspace per brand

Each workspace keeps its content, connected accounts, brand voice, and approval links completely separate from every other workspace. Nothing you set up for one client brand shows up in another, so it is safe to run several client brands from the same Vidflow account. See Workspaces and brand spaces for the full list of what a workspace isolates.

#Adding more brand spaces

Every account includes one workspace on any plan, including Free. Creating a second workspace requires the Agency plan. Beyond the one workspace Agency includes, owners can purchase additional brand spaces as a metered add-on at $49/month each, adjusted from the Billing page as you need them.

  1. Open Billing & plan (avatar, Account settings, Manage plan & billing).
  2. On the Agency plan card, use the Extra brand spaces stepper to set how many additional spaces you want.
  3. Click Update brand spaces (or Switch to Agency if you are not on it yet).

Each additional brand space adds its own episode allowance to your account total (see Usage limits and monthly allowances), and its own working-seat allowance for that workspace.

Note

Note: If you try to create a workspace beyond what your plan allows, Vidflow shows an in-app message telling you what to do next: upgrade to Agency, or add a brand space if you are already on Agency.

#Copying content between workspaces

If you have already built out one client's content and want to reuse pieces of it for another brand, you do not need to rebuild from scratch. From the Content Library, select items and open Bulk Manage, then choose Copy Content to Other Workspaces. Pick the destination workspace; the selected items are copied into it, leaving the originals in place. See Bulk actions for the full steps.

Bulk actions, including this one, are available on the Pro and Agency plans. See Plans and features.

#Troubleshooting

The Agency add-on stepper won't let me confirm. The button only activates once the quantity you selected differs from what is currently on your subscription, or if you are switching to Agency for the first time.

A client's content showed up in the wrong workspace. This should not happen through normal use; every action is scoped to whichever workspace is active in the switcher. If you see cross-brand content, switch workspaces to confirm which one is actually selected before reporting it, then contact support@vidflow.io.
