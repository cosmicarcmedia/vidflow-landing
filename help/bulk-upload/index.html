Bulk upload and CSV import

Updated July 9, 2026

The Bulk Upload page does two jobs. A drag-and-drop wizard turns a batch of files into library items with AI titles and captions, and a CSV importer creates posts you have already written.

#The Bulk Upload wizard

Note

Note: The wizard is included on the Pro and Agency plans. The CSV importer further down the page works on any plan. See Plans and features.

The wizard runs in three steps: Drop Files, Review, and Process.

  1. Click Make Content, then Bulk Upload.
  2. Drag your files in, or click to browse. Video, audio, images, plain text, markdown, and CSV files are supported.
  3. Set Link to source episode (optional but recommended). Linking to an episode gives the AI context to write better titles and captions. If you stage clips without one, Vidflow shows a Missing Episode Context warning before it processes them.
  4. Click Review Files, check the list, then click Process All.

Vidflow uploads the files, writes an AI title, caption, and tags for each one, and adds everything to your Content Library. When it finishes, use View in Library to see the results or Upload More to run another batch.

#Import posts from a CSV

The Import from spreadsheet (CSV) card on the same page works on any plan. Use it to queue weeks of posts at once.

  1. Click Template to download vidflow-import-template.csv and fill it in.
  2. Click Import CSV and drop your file in.
  3. Check the preview, then click Import. The button shows how many posts are ready.

CSV columns

ColumnWhat it does
titleOptional title for the post.
captionThe post text. Required. Rows without a caption are skipped, and the preview shows how many.
platformsTarget platforms: instagram, facebook, tiktok, youtube, linkedin, x (twitter also works as an alias for x). Separate multiple platforms with a semicolon.
tagsTags to apply. Separate multiple tags with a semicolon.
scheduledAtA date and time such as 2026-07-01 09:00. Rows with a valid date import as scheduled posts. Rows without one import as drafts.
mediaUrlA public link (starting with http or https) to an image to attach.

#Troubleshooting

The dropzone shows an upgrade prompt. The drag-and-drop wizard requires the Pro plan or higher. The CSV importer on the same page still works on any plan.

"That CSV has no data rows." The file needs a header row plus at least one content row. Start from the downloadable template.

"No rows with a caption/body were found." Every row needs text in the caption column. Rows without one are skipped.

Platforms did not apply. Platform names must match the list above exactly, separated by semicolons.
