Help / Workspaces and team

Invite team members

Updated July 9, 2026

Bring teammates into a workspace so more than one person can work on a brand's content.

Note

Note: Team access is based on seats. Every workspace includes one working seat, which the owner uses, and each plan includes a set number of working seats, so adding Admin or Brand Manager teammates starts on the Pro plan. Client reviewers never use a seat. On the Free plan the invite controls are locked. See Plans and features.

#Send an invite

  1. Open Workspace Settings and go to the Team tab.
  2. In the Invite a teammate card, enter the person's email address.
  3. Choose a role: Admin, Brand Manager, or Client.
  4. Click Send invite.

The invite appears in a pending list on the same card, with a Revoke option if you need to cancel it before it is accepted.

#Roles

  • Admin and Brand Manager are working team members. They count toward your plan's seat limit for this workspace, and the workspace owner always counts as one seat.
  • Client is the reviewer role, meant for people who only need to approve or comment on content. Client seats are always free and never count against your seat limit, no matter how many you add.

How many Admin and Brand Manager seats your plan includes varies by plan. See Plans and features for exact seat counts.

#How the invitee joins

An invited person is added automatically the first time they sign in with the invited email address. If they already have a Vidflow account under that email, they are added to the workspace immediately instead of waiting for a next sign-in.

Important

Important: Auto-join on first sign-in currently works when the person signs in with Google. If they sign in with email and password before that, they may not land in the workspace automatically. If that happens, ask a workspace admin to send the invite again, or add them from the Team tab.
