Help / Workspaces and team

Invite team members

Updated July 10, 2026

Bring teammates into a workspace so more than one person can work on a brand's content. Reach for this article when you are building out an agency team, giving a client review-only access, or figuring out why an invite did not go through.

#How it works

Every workspace member has exactly one role, checked on the server before any settings change, invite, or admin action: admin (full access), brand_manager (content and publishing access, no team or danger-zone access), or client (view and approve only). Sending an invite whitelists the email so it can sign in, writes a pending invite row, and emails the person a link. If they already have a Vidflow account under that email, they are added to the workspace immediately instead of waiting on the email.

Note

Note: Team access is based on seats. The workspace owner always counts as one seat, and each plan includes a set number of additional working seats (Admin + Brand Manager combined) per workspace: Free and Starter include 1 (the owner only), Pro includes 2, Agency includes 10 per brand space. Client reviewer seats are always free and unlimited. See Plans and features.

#Send an invite

  1. Open Workspace Settings and go to the Team tab.
  2. In the Invite a teammate card, enter the person's email address.
  3. Choose a role: Admin, Brand Manager, or Client.
  4. Click Send invite.

Sending an invite requires admin access on the workspace. If the invite email cannot be sent (for example email is not configured), Vidflow still creates the invite and shows you the sign-in link to pass along yourself; the person is added automatically the moment they sign in with that email.

The invite appears in a Pending invites list on the same card, with a Revoke option if you need to cancel it before it is accepted.

#Roles

  • Admin: full access to a workspace, including Workspace Settings, the Team tab, billing-adjacent actions, and the Danger Zone (freeze or delete the workspace).
  • Brand Manager: can create and manage content, episodes, clips, and publishing, but cannot open the Team tab, invite others, or reach the Danger Zone.
  • Client: the reviewer role, meant for people who only need to approve or comment on content through an approval link. Client seats are always free and never count against your seat limit, no matter how many you add.

Admin and Brand Manager both count toward your plan's working-seat limit for this workspace; Client never does. See Plans and features for exact seat counts per plan.

#How the invitee joins

An invited person is added automatically the first time they sign in with the invited email address. If they already have a Vidflow account under that email, they are added to the workspace immediately when you send the invite, no sign-in wait required.

Important

Important: Auto-join on first sign-in currently works when the person signs in with Google. If they sign in with email and password before that, they may not land in the workspace automatically. If that happens, ask a workspace admin to send the invite again, or add them from the Team tab once they have an account.

#Troubleshooting

Send invite is greyed out or missing. Only admins can send invites; Brand Managers and Clients do not see the full Team tab controls. Ask a workspace admin to send it, or to promote you to admin.

I hit "This brand space is at its seat limit." You are out of working seats for Admin/Brand Manager on this plan. Remove an existing member, upgrade the plan, or (on Agency) add another brand space. Client invites are never blocked by this.

The invited person still cannot get in. Confirm they are signing in with the exact email address the invite was sent to, and try Google sign-in first since that is what triggers auto-join today.
