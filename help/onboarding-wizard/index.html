Set up your brand with the onboarding wizard

Updated July 10, 2026

The onboarding wizard is the first thing a new workspace sees. It captures your brand name, audience, voice, and visual style in five short steps, then feeds every one of those answers straight into the AI tools you use later, so your first clips, posts, and images already sound like you instead of a generic assistant.

#How it works

Every workspace has an onboardedAt flag that starts empty. Until it is set, Vidflow redirects you into the wizard the moment you land on any app page. Finishing the wizard, or skipping it, sets that flag and the redirect stops firing. Everything you enter is saved directly to your workspace's Brand Settings: brand basics, target audience, brand voice guidelines, your voice profile, and your image style prompt, so nothing here is a separate copy that later needs merging in.

#The five steps

  1. Welcome. A fork in the road: Fill it out myself walks you through the remaining steps by hand. Use my AI to fill this out is where the AI Shortcut lives, copy a ready-made prompt into ChatGPT, Claude, or another assistant, paste its answer back, and Vidflow parses it straight into your brand profile. See Get the most out of Vidflow by looping in your AI for the full walkthrough of that shortcut, including where to find it if it isn't showing on this step yet.
  2. Who you are. Your brand name, an optional website, and an optional industry. If you have a YouTube channel, paste its URL or @handle into Import from YouTube and click Import, Vidflow pulls your logo, brand color, and industry from the channel in one click.
  3. Your voice and audience. A target audience description and free-text brand voice guidelines. Below that, Analyze my voice builds a voice profile: Analyze from my channel if you imported a YouTube channel in the previous step, or paste 3 to 5 real captions, posts, or a transcript (minimum 50 characters) and click Build from samples.
  4. Your look. Pick a starting preset (Clean, Bold, Editorial, Minimal, or Photographic) or write your own image style prompt from scratch, plus an optional logo URL and brand color.
  5. Review and finish. Every field you filled in is listed for a final check, with a "from your AI" badge on anything the AI Shortcut supplied. Click Back to change anything, or Looks good, build my first pack to save it all and move to your first episode.

You can go back and forth between steps at any point with the Back and Next buttons, and a Skip for now link at the top of every step jumps straight past the whole thing.

#Skipping the wizard

Skipping is a real option, not a dead end. Skip for now saves nothing but still marks the workspace as onboarded, so you land in the normal app instead of getting redirected back into the wizard. Nothing is lost: every field the wizard would have set lives in the same Brand Settings screens, so you can fill in your brand voice, audience, and image style whenever you're ready.

#Revisiting the wizard later

The wizard is not a one-time gate. Open /app/onboarding directly at any time to run through it again, whether you skipped it originally or just want to redo your answers. Reopening it prefills your current workspace name, website, industry, and target audience so you are editing forward, not starting from a blank slate. You can also edit any of these fields individually without the wizard at all, from Workspace Settings' Brand and AI & Voice tabs.

#How this feeds everything else

Nothing you enter here is wizard-only. It writes to the exact same brand settings that every generator reads from:

  • Your target audience and brand voice guidelines shape every Magic Text Post, script, blog post, and caption Vidflow writes.
  • Your voice profile (from a YouTube channel analysis or pasted samples) is the same shape Vidflow uses everywhere else it studies your voice, so it carries straight into captions, posts, and scripts.
  • Your image style prompt becomes the default look for AI-generated images and quote cards.
  • Your logo and brand color show up anywhere the workspace's branding appears.

Finishing the wizard hands you straight to Your first content pack, where you add your first episode and watch Vidflow generate content from everything you just set up.

#Get better results

  • Import your YouTube channel in step 2 before analyzing your voice in step 3, Analyze from my channel gives a fuller voice profile than a handful of pasted samples.
  • If you don't have a channel to import, paste more than the 50-character minimum for Build from samples, three to five real captions or a full transcript gives the AI more to work with than a couple of sentences.
  • Use the AI Shortcut on step 1 if you already talk to ChatGPT or Claude about your business, it usually saves you from typing out your voice and audience from scratch.
  • Be specific in your image style prompt: mention colors, mood, lighting, and composition rather than a single adjective. The presets are a starting point, keep editing after you pick one.
  • Fill in target audience even if you skip everything else, it is the single field that most changes how the AI writes for you later.

#Troubleshooting

I keep getting sent back to the wizard. The workspace has not been onboarded yet. Either finish the wizard or click Skip for now at the top of any step, either one sets the flag that stops the redirect.

The AI Shortcut paste did not fill anything in. See Get the most out of Vidflow by looping in your AI for how to retry the paste or fall back to filling the wizard in manually.

I want to redo my answers after finishing. Open /app/onboarding again. It prefills what it can from your current workspace, and finishing it again simply overwrites the fields you change.
