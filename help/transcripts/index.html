Transcripts

Updated July 9, 2026

Every episode gets a transcript, and much of Vidflow works from it: clips, captions, and AI writing all read the transcript. Transcription starts automatically after an upload or import.

#How transcripts are generated

  • Uploaded files are transcribed with AI speech-to-text.
  • Episodes imported from YouTube pull the video's transcript instead.
  • Vidflow transcribes in English today.
Note

Note: Transcribing an upload counts as one AI generation against your plan's monthly allowance. See Usage limits and monthly allowances.

#Track transcription status

A transcript moves through pending, processing, and completed states, or failed if something went wrong. The episode shows a status badge. While a transcription runs, the notifications bell in the header lists it as a Transcription job and links to your episodes.

#Start or retry transcription

If an episode shows "Transcript not yet generated", open it and click Transcribe Now. After a failed attempt, the same button retries. It is disabled while a transcription is already running.

#Re-pull or download a transcript

Open the episode from your Content Library and go to the Transcript tab:

  • Pull transcript fetches a transcript for an episode that has none yet.
  • Re-pull replaces the existing transcript. It pulls fresh YouTube captions or re-runs speech-to-text on the media.
  • Download .txt saves the transcript as a plain text file.

#What transcripts power

  • Clip generation reads the transcript to find the strongest moments. See Generate clips from a long video.
  • Magic Text Posts turns an episode transcript into a batch of ready-to-publish posts. See Magic Text Posts.
  • Caption and title writing across the app uses the transcript for context.

#Troubleshooting

"Transcription failed." Open the episode and click Transcribe Now to retry.

"Couldn't pull a transcript for this YouTube video." The video may not have captions available. Try Re-pull later, or upload the file itself so Vidflow can transcribe the audio directly.

"Monthly AI generation limit reached." Transcription shares your plan's monthly AI allowance. See Usage limits and monthly allowances for what to do at the cap.
